Process Instrumentation

Accurate measurement is the foundation of process control. We supply a full range of field instruments from world-class manufacturers including Endress+Hauser, WIKA, Pepperl+Fuchs, and Honeywell:

  • Flow meters — electromagnetic, vortex, Coriolis, and ultrasonic
  • Pressure transmitters and gauges for up to 1,000 bar
  • Level measurement — radar, guided wave, ultrasonic, and hydrostatic
  • Temperature sensors — thermocouples, RTDs, and transmitters
  • pH, conductivity, and analytical instruments

PLCs, HMIs & SCADA Systems

From simple relay logic replacements to complex distributed control systems, our automation team specifies and supplies Siemens SIMATIC, Allen-Bradley / Rockwell Automation, and Omron programmable logic controllers. We pair these with intuitive HMI panels and SCADA software to give your operators full visibility of the process from a single screen.

Safety Systems & Hazardous Area Equipment

Industrial safety is non-negotiable. We supply safety-rated PLCs, emergency stop systems, safety light curtains, and intrinsically safe field devices from Pepperl+Fuchs and Warom for use in Zone 1 and Zone 2 hazardous areas — essential for refineries, chemical plants, and flour mills.

Valve Actuation & Control

Our Rotork and Emerson electric, pneumatic, and hydraulic actuators provide reliable valve control in the most demanding applications — from high-cycle on-off duties to modulating control on critical process lines. We also supply positioners, limit switches, and solenoid valve manifolds.

The IPATECHS Automation Advantage

We don't just supply hardware. Our team provides loop tuning, commissioning support, and operator training to ensure your automation investment delivers measurable results — reduced waste, lower energy consumption, improved product quality, and safer working conditions.
