Service Overview

Clean Water for Tanzanian Communities and Industries

Access to clean water and effective wastewater treatment are fundamental to public health, industrial productivity, and environmental protection. IPATECHS supplies water and wastewater treatment solutions to municipal water authorities, industrial plants, and construction projects across Tanzania.

We provide complete solutions: pumps, aeration equipment, chemical dosing systems, filtration media, pipework, valves, instrumentation, and treatment plant spare parts. Our technical team assists with equipment selection, hydraulic calculations, and system integration.
