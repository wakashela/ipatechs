Industrial Alloys & Specialty Steels

Our alloy portfolio covers the full spectrum of industrial metallurgy. We supply stainless steel in grades 304, 304L, 316, 316L, 321, and 904L for general corrosion service, alongside duplex (2205, 2507) and super duplex stainless steels that combine high strength with exceptional chloride pitting resistance — critical for Tanzania’s coastal and offshore installations. For aggressive chemical processing environments we stock nickel alloys including Inconel 600, 625, and 718; Hastelloy C-276 and C-22; and Monel 400 and K-500. These materials serve the chemical plants, desalination units, and acid handling systems that operate across industrial zones in Dar es Salaam and Mtwara.

High-Temperature & Wear-Resistant Materials

Process industries in Tanzania — from the cement kilns of Tanga and Dangote to the boilers at sugar factories in Kilombero and Kagera — rely on materials that retain structural integrity at elevated temperatures. IPATECHS supplies high-temperature alloys including Incoloy 800H/HT, 253 MA, and RA330 for furnace components, radiant tubes, and heat-treatment fixtures. We also provide refractory anchor systems and thermal expansion alloys for kiln shell maintenance. For the mining and quarrying sector, our wear-resistant plate program covers Hardox, Brinar, and Dillidur in thicknesses from 6 mm to 160 mm, along with chromium carbide overlay (CCO) plate for extreme abrasion. These materials extend liner life in chutes, hoppers, truck beds, and crusher housings at operations like Geita Gold Mine, Bulyanhulu, and North Mara.

Structural Steel & Special Sections

IPATECHS supplies structural steel sections for construction, fabrication, and infrastructure projects including beams (I-beams, H-beams, wide flange), channels (U-channel, C-channel, parallel flange channel), angles (equal and unequal leg), flat bars, square bars, and round bars in grades S235, S275, S355, and weathering steel (Corten A/B). All structural steel is available with mill test certificates and can be supplied cut-to-length, shot-blasted, and primed to project specification.
