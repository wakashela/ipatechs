Frequently Asked Questions

HART vs Fieldbus — which protocol should I choose?

HART is the industry standard for 4-20mA loops, superimposing digital communication on the analogue signal — ideal for brownfield upgrades and simple point-to-point measurement. Foundation Fieldbus and PROFIBUS PA are fully digital fieldbus protocols that support multi-drop wiring and advanced device diagnostics. For new greenfield plants, we typically recommend Foundation Fieldbus; for retrofits and smaller installations, HART-capable instruments offer the best cost-benefit ratio.

Can you supply ATEX-certified instruments for hazardous areas?

Yes. We supply ATEX, IECEx, and FM-approved pressure transmitters, temperature sensors, and level transmitters for Zone 0, 1, and 2 (gas) and Zone 20, 21, and 22 (dust) hazardous areas. Our WIKA and Emerson instruments include intrinsically safe (Ex ia), flameproof (Ex d), and increased safety (Ex e) protection methods. Intrinsically safe barriers and isolators from Pepperl+Fuchs and MTL complete each hazardous-area installation.

Do you offer instrument calibration services in Tanzania?

Every instrument we supply can be delivered with a traceable calibration certificate. We also provide on-site calibration services using Fluke documenting process calibrators and deadweight testers. Our technicians can calibrate pressure transmitters (0-600 bar), temperature transmitters (RTD and thermocouple), and 4-20mA loops against certified reference standards. Third-party ISO 17025 calibration is also available on request.

Can process instruments integrate with my existing PLC or DCS?

Absolutely. Our instrumentation team has experience integrating WIKA, Emerson, and Endress+Hauser instruments with Siemens, Allen-Bradley, Schneider, ABB, Honeywell, and Yokogawa control systems. We configure device parameters, provide instrument-specific DD/EDDL/DTM files, and support HART multiplexer, HART-IP, and fieldbus gateway architectures. Whether you're replacing a single transmitter or instrumenting a complete plant, we ensure seamless communication between your field devices and your control room.
