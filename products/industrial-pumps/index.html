Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Grundfos CR and CRN?

CR pumps feature cast iron construction for standard water and non-aggressive fluid applications. CRN pumps use stainless steel (AISI 316) for all wetted parts, making them the correct choice for aggressive, corrosive, or high-purity fluids found in chemical processing and pharmaceutical operations.

What flow rates can LEWA metering pumps handle?

LEWA ecoflow and modular diaphragm metering pumps cover flow rates from 0.1 to 7200 L/h depending on the model and configuration. Operating pressures range from atmospheric to 1200 bar. We help you select the correct pump head size, diaphragm material, and drive configuration for your specific process requirements.

How do I select the right pump for my application?

Pump selection starts with four parameters: required flow rate (m3/hr), total dynamic head (metres), fluid properties (viscosity, temperature, corrosiveness, solids content), and available NPSH at the pump suction. Our engineers perform system curve analysis, NPSH margin verification, and material compatibility checks to ensure the pump operates within its recommended duty range — avoiding cavitation, vibration, and premature wear.
