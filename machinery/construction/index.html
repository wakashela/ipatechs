Frequently Asked Questions

Do you supply new or used construction machinery?

Both. We supply brand-new construction machinery direct from manufacturers, as well as quality used and refurbished equipment — all inspected and verified before sale.

What warranty comes with the machines?

New machines come with full manufacturer warranty (typically 12–24 months or 2,000–5,000 hours depending on brand and model). Used and refurbished machines come with a 3–6 month IPATECHS warranty covering major components.

How long does delivery take?

Machines already in our Dar es Salaam stock can be delivered within 48–72 hours anywhere in Tanzania. Factory orders typically take 8–16 weeks depending on manufacturer lead time and shipping from origin.

Do you ship construction machines outside Tanzania?

Yes. We regularly deliver construction machinery to Kenya, Uganda, Rwanda, Zambia, Mozambique, and the DRC. Our logistics team handles all cross-border documentation, duties, and transport.

Can I see the machine before buying?

Absolutely. We encourage pre-purchase inspections. For stock machines in Dar es Salaam, we can arrange a viewing at our yard. For factory orders, we can connect you with the manufacturer's local representative or arrange a video walkaround.
