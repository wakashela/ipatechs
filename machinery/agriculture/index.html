Service Overview

Mechanising Tanzanian Agriculture

Agriculture employs over 65% of Tanzania's workforce and contributes nearly 30% of GDP. The SAGCOT corridor — stretching from Morogoro through Iringa to Mbeya — represents one of Africa's most ambitious agricultural development programmes. Yet Tanzania's mechanisation rate remains among the lowest on the continent. IPATECHS is changing that, supplying the tractors, harvesters, planters, and irrigation systems that Tanzanian farmers need to scale from subsistence to commercial production.

From a 25 HP compact tractor for a smallholder in Njombe to a 400 HP articulated four-track for a sugar estate in Kilombero, our agricultural machinery division delivers the right equipment — new and used — with warranty, spare parts, and service support.

New agricultural machines come with full manufacturer warranty (typically 12–24 months). Used and refurbished machines come with a 3–6 month IPATECHS warranty covering major drivetrain and hydraulic components.
