Service Overview

Equipping East Africa's Gateway Ports

Tanzania's ports are the gateway to East and Central Africa. The Dar es Salaam port handles over 95% of Tanzania's international trade and serves six landlocked neighbours. With the new TICTS container terminal, the Mtwara deep-water port expansion, and the SGR freight terminals coming online, demand for modern port handling equipment has never been higher. IPATECHS supplies the cranes, reach stackers, and terminal tractors that keep cargo moving.

Whether you're equipping a container terminal in Dar es Salaam, a bulk handling facility in Tanga, or an inland container depot in Dodoma, our port handling division delivers the right machinery — new, used, or refurbished — with full commissioning and after-sales support.
