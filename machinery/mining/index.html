Frequently Asked Questions

Do you supply new or used mining machinery?

Both. We supply brand-new mining machinery direct from manufacturers, as well as quality used and refurbished equipment — all inspected and verified before sale. We also offer certified rebuild programmes for major components.

What warranty comes with the machines?

New machines come with full manufacturer warranty (typically 12–24 months or 2,000–5,000 hours depending on brand and model). Used and refurbished machines come with a 3–6 month IPATECHS warranty covering major components.

Can you ship heavy mining equipment to remote mine sites?

Yes. We specialise in heavy-lift logistics to remote sites. We handle oversized transport permits, escort vehicles, bridge assessments, and route planning. Our team has delivered equipment to Geita, North Mara, and Bulyanhulu — some of the most challenging logistics routes in Tanzania.

Do you supply both surface and underground equipment?

Yes. We supply equipment for both open-pit (surface) and underground mining operations — from ultra-class haul trucks and rope shovels for open pits to LHDs, jumbos, and bolters for underground development and production.

What about spare parts and ongoing support?

We maintain a dedicated mining machinery parts inventory in Dar es Salaam, stocking high-wear components including filters, GET (ground-engaging tools), undercarriage parts, and hydraulic seals. For rebuild programmes, we can supply complete engine, transmission, and final drive overhaul kits with factory support.
