ABOUT IPATECHS

IPATECHS COMPANY LIMITED is a globally recognized leader in the supply and distribution of industrial spare parts, engineering solutions, and construction machinery. Founded in 2019 in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania, we have rapidly grown into East Africa's most trusted engineering solutions partner.

With a commitment to quality and innovation, we specialize in providing products and services across diverse industries in Tanzania and the wider East African region. As a trusted partner to both multinational corporations and local enterprises, we bridge the gap between world-class manufacturers and the industries that depend on their products.

Our deep technical expertise, combined with robust logistics capabilities and a team of passionate engineers, ensures our clients receive exactly what they need — on time, on specification, and at competitive pricing. We don't just supply parts; we deliver uptime and peace of mind.
