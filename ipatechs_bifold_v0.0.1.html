Company Overview
ABOUT IPATECHS

IPATECHS COMPANY LIMITED is a globally recognized leader in the supply and distribution of industrial spare parts, engineering solutions, and construction machinery. Founded in 2019 in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania, we have rapidly grown into East Africa's most trusted engineering solutions partner.

With a commitment to quality and innovation, we specialize in providing products and services across diverse industries in Tanzania and the wider East African region. As a trusted partner to both multinational corporations and local enterprises, we bridge the gap between world-class manufacturers and the industries that depend on their products.

Our deep technical expertise, combined with robust logistics capabilities and a team of passionate engineers, ensures our clients receive exactly what they need — on time, on specification, and at competitive pricing. We don't just supply parts; we deliver uptime and peace of mind.

Our Process
How We Deliver Results
OUR PROCESS
01

Enquiry & Requirement Analysis

Clients contact us via WhatsApp, email, or phone with part numbers, specifications, or equipment descriptions. Our engineers analyse requirements and confirm the correct product, grade, and quantity — preventing costly ordering errors before they happen.

02

Same-Day Technical Quotation

We respond with a detailed written quotation within 2 hours on business days. Our quotes include product specifications, brand certification, lead time, delivery terms, and pricing — fully transparent, no hidden charges, valid for 30 days.

03

Sourcing & Quality Verification

Orders are placed through authorised manufacturer channels. On receipt at our Dar es Salaam warehouse, every shipment is inspected for packaging integrity, hologram verification, batch numbering, and dimensional accuracy before client dispatch.

04

Delivery & Logistics

Within Dar es Salaam, same-day or next-morning delivery is standard. Upcountry deliveries to Mwanza, Arusha, Mbeya, and Dodoma reach clients within 24–72 hours. International orders to Kenya, Uganda, Rwanda, and Zambia are coordinated with proven freight partners.

05

After-Sales Support & Partnership

Our relationship continues after delivery. We provide installation guidance, failure analysis, warranty processing, and preventive maintenance programme recommendations — building the long-term partnerships that define the IPATECHS difference.
