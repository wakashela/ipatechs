Pipeline Components & Fittings

The backbone of any oil & gas installation is its piping system, and IPATECHS supplies a comprehensive range of pipeline parts Tanzania. Our inventory covers seamless and welded pipe in carbon steel (ASTM A106 Gr. B, API 5L Gr. B/X42–X70), stainless steel (304/304L, 316/316L), and duplex stainless for corrosive service. We supply butt-weld fittings (long-radius and short-radius elbows, equal and reducing tees, concentric and eccentric reducers, caps, stub ends) and forged fittings (socket weld and threaded elbows, tees, couplings, unions, caps, nipples, and crosses) manufactured to ASME B16.9, B16.11, and MSS-SP standards. For flange connections, we provide weld neck, slip-on, blind, socket weld, lap joint, threaded, and spectacle blinds in pressure classes from 150# through 2500#, manufactured to ASME B16.5 (up to 24") and B16.47 Series A/B (26" and above). All gaskets are supplied to match — spiral wound, ring type joint (RTJ), and non-metallic (compressed fibre, PTFE) per ASME B16.20 — along with B7/B7M, B8/B8M, and B16 stud bolts and nuts.

Industrial Valves for Oil & Gas

IPATECHS supplies the full range of valves required across the petroleum and gas value chain. We provide ball valves (floating and trunnion-mounted, soft-seated and metal-seated) for on/off isolation service, gate valves (rising stem and non-rising stem, solid wedge and flexible wedge) for mainline isolation, globe valves for throttling and flow regulation, check valves (swing, piston, dual-plate, and nozzle types) for backflow prevention, and safety relief valves (spring-loaded and pilot-operated) for overpressure protection. All valves are available in cast and forged construction with end connections including flanged, butt-weld, socket weld, and threaded, in pressure ratings from Class 150 to Class 2500. For sour service applications in gas fields like Mnazi Bay, we can specify materials compliant with NACE MR0175 to prevent sulphide stress cracking. We also source API 6D pipeline valves and API 6A wellhead components through our international supply partnerships.

Honeywell Gas Detection & Safety Systems

Safety is non-negotiable in the oil & gas industry, and IPATECHS supplies Honeywell gas detection and safety systems to protect personnel, assets, and the environment. Our Honeywell portfolio includes the Searchpoint Optima Plus and Searchline Excel infrared gas detectors for hydrocarbon monitoring, Sensepoint XCD and XNX Universal Transmitters for toxic and combustible gas detection, MultiTox and MicroMAX fixed gas detectors for hydrogen sulphide (H₂S) and carbon monoxide, and the Fire & Gas (F&G) system controllers that integrate detection, alarm, and executive action. We also supply flame detectors (UV, IR, and combined UV/IR), manual call points, and audible/visual alarm devices rated for hazardous area zones. These systems are critical infrastructure for gas processing plants, compressor stations along the Mtwara–Dar pipeline, and storage terminals handling LPG and LNG.

Pressure Vessels, Heat Exchangers & Pumps

Beyond piping and valves, IPATECHS supplies major process equipment for the petroleum sector. We source pressure vessels and storage tanks designed and fabricated to ASME Section VIII Division 1 (and Division 2 where required), with materials including carbon steel, stainless steel, and clad plate for corrosive service. Our heat exchanger supply covers shell-and-tube (BEM, BEU, AES types), plate, and air-cooled exchangers with thermal design to TEMA standards — essential for gas cooling at compressor stations and product cooling in refinery operations. For fluid transfer, we provide centrifugal pumps (API 610 for refinery service, end-suction and split-case designs), positive displacement pumps (gear, screw, and diaphragm types for metering and transfer), and associated mechanical seals, couplings, and baseplates. These pump packages are available with electric motor or diesel engine drivers, suited to both grid-connected and remote wellhead locations.

Instrumentation for Oil & Gas

Accurate measurement and control are fundamental to safe, efficient hydrocarbon operations. IPATECHS supplies process instrumentation including pressure transmitters (gauge and differential), flow meters (Coriolis, ultrasonic, vortex, turbine, and orifice-based), level transmitters (guided wave radar, non-contact radar, displacer, magnetic level gauges), and temperature sensors (RTDs, thermocouples, temperature transmitters with thermowells). These instruments are available in intrinsically safe and explosion-proof configurations for Zone 0, 1, and 2 hazardous areas, with HART, Foundation Fieldbus, and wireless HART communication protocols. Our instrumentation packages integrate directly with the PLC/SCADA control systems covered by our Automation Engineering division, enabling complete digital field-to-control-room solutions for gas metering stations, custody transfer points, and pipeline monitoring. We also partner with our Power & Energy Solutions team to provide generator backup packages for remote wellhead and valve station sites.
