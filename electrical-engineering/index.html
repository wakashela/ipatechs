Low & Medium Voltage Switchgear

Switchgear is the first line of defence in any electrical distribution system — and the consequences of under-specifying it can be fatal. IPATECHS supplies switchgear from the three global leaders trusted by Tanzania's electrical engineering community:

  • Schneider Electric — MasterPacT MTZ air circuit breakers (ACBs) up to 6300 A, Compact NSX moulded-case circuit breakers (MCCBs) with Micrologic trip units, Okken and BlokSeT low-voltage switchboards, and SM6/WS-G gas-insulated medium-voltage switchgear up to 36 kV. Schneider's EcoStruxure platform provides built-in power monitoring, enabling your plant to track energy consumption by feeder.
  • Siemens — SENTRON 3WL ACBs, 3VA MCCBs with integrated metering and communication, SIVACON S8 LV switchboards, and 8DJH gas-insulated MV ring main units. Siemens' Totally Integrated Power (TIP) philosophy ensures seamless coordination between protection devices from incomer to final circuit.
  • ABB — Emax 2 ACBs with Ekip trip units, Tmax XT MCCBs, MNS and System pro E power LV switchboards, and UniGear ZS1 MV air-insulated switchgear panels. ABB's RELION protection relays provide comprehensive feeder, motor, and transformer protection for medium-voltage applications.

We also supply automatic transfer switches (ATS) from Socomec and ASCO, essential for facilities with standby generators — a common configuration across Tanzania where grid interruptions remain a daily operational reality. As the switchgear supplier Tanzania industries rely on, we ensure every panel we deliver is type-tested to IEC 61439 and comes with full short-circuit coordination studies when required.

Variable Frequency Drives (VFDs)

A variable frequency drive can reduce a pump or fan's energy consumption by 20 to 50 percent compared to throttling or on/off control — often delivering a payback period of under two years. For Tanzania's industries where electricity is one of the largest operational costs, the business case for VFDs is undeniable. IPATECHS is a leading VFD supplier East Africa, offering:

  • ABB ACS880 & ACS580 Series — ABB's industrial drives cover 0.55 kW to 5,600 kW in wall-mounted, cabinet-built, and module configurations. Direct torque control (DTC) provides precise speed and torque regulation without encoder feedback — ideal for demanding applications like crane hoists, extruders, and winding machines. Built-in Safe Torque Off (STO) and ATEX-certified motor over-temperature protection simplify compliance with safety standards.
  • Siemens SINAMICS G120 & S120 — The G120 modular drive is the workhorse for standard pump and fan applications, while the S120 servo drive platform handles high-dynamic positioning tasks in packaging, printing, and machine tool applications. Both integrate natively with Siemens SIMATIC PLCs via PROFINET.
  • Schneider Electric Altivar Process & Altivar Machine — ATV600 series for heavy-duty process applications from 0.75 kW to 2,600 kW, and ATV320 for compact machine-mounted drives. Both support Ethernet/IP and Modbus TCP for plant-wide connectivity.

Every VFD we supply includes application-specific parameterisation. We don't just ship a drive in factory-default mode — we configure acceleration ramps, current limits, switching frequencies, motor protection parameters, and communication settings for your actual motor and application. For retrofits on existing DOL starters, we supply harmonic mitigation (line reactors, passive filters, or active front-end drives) to prevent nuisance tripping of upstream protection and to comply with IEEE 519 harmonic limits.

  • WEG & Bharat Bijlee Motors — Cost-effective alternatives for less demanding applications with excellent availability for the African market. Widely used in Tanzania's water pumping stations, agricultural processing, and general manufacturing.

Motor Control Centres (MCCs) & Control Panels

The motor control centre is the nerve centre of any industrial plant. IPATECHS designs and supplies MCCs in Form 2, Form 3, and Form 4 segregation to meet the required level of operational safety and maintenance accessibility. Each MCC is built around your specific motor list and integrates:

  • DOL, star-delta, soft-starter, and VFD starters — each configured with correctly rated circuit breakers, contactors, overload relays, and control wiring
  • Incomer and bus-coupler sections with ACBs or MCCBs for flexible power distribution within the MCC
  • Intelligent motor management relays (ABB UMC100, Siemens SIMOCODE pro) providing real-time current, voltage, power, and thermal capacity data to the plant SCADA

PLC Systems & Industrial Automation

Programmable logic controllers form the brain of modern industrial automation. IPATECHS supplies and supports the two dominant PLC ecosystems in East Africa:

  • Siemens SIMATIC S7-1200 & S7-1500 — The S7-1200 is ideal for compact machine control and small process skids, while the S7-1500 handles large-scale plant automation with redundancy, safety-integrated functions, and high-speed backplane communication.
