Our Industrial Instrumentation Range

From field devices to control room displays, we deliver the instruments that give you process visibility:

Pressure and Temperature Measurement

WIKA pressure transmitters (A-10, S-20, P-30), pressure gauges (bourdon tube and diaphragm seal), and thermometers. Emerson Rosemount pressure and temperature transmitters with HART and Foundation Fieldbus communication. Differential pressure transmitters for flow and level measurement. Thermowells, siphons, and manifold valves for complete installations.

Flow and Level Measurement

Endress+Hauser electromagnetic (Promag), Coriolis (Promass), vortex (Prowirl), and ultrasonic (Prosonic Flow) flow meters for liquids, gases, and steam. Orifice plates, venturi tubes, and averaging pitot tubes for DP-based flow. Radar (guided wave and free-space), ultrasonic, capacitance, and hydrostatic level transmitters for tanks, silos, and open channels.

Endress+Hauser Emerson WIKA Siemens
