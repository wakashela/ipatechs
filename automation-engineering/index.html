Variable Frequency Drives & Motor Control

Electric motors consume approximately 45% of global industrial electricity, and VFDs are the single most effective technology for reducing that consumption. We supply Siemens SINAMICS G120 and G120X drives (0.55 kW to 250 kW), ABB ACS580 and ACS880 general-purpose and industrial drives, and Rockwell PowerFlex 525 and 755 drives for applications ranging from simple pump and fan speed control to coordinated multi-axis conveyor systems. Our drives come in IP20 (panel-mount), IP55, and IP66 enclosures suitable for everything from clean control rooms to dusty cement plant environments. We stock braking resistors, DC chokes, output reactors for long motor cable runs, and EMC filters for compliance with electromagnetic compatibility standards. For critical processes, we configure redundant drive systems with automatic changeover and synchronisation.
