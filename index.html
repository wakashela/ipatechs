Who We Are

IN ENGINEERING

IPATECHS COMPANY LIMITED is a globally recognized leader in the supply and distribution of industrial spare parts, engineering solutions, and construction machinery. With a commitment to quality and innovation, we specialize in providing products and services across diverse industries in Tanzania and the wider East African region.

  • Mechanical & Electrical Engineering
  • Material & Petroleum Engineering
  • Automation, Mining & Power Systems
  • Agricultural & Construction Sector

Our mission is to empower industries with reliable, world-class engineering solutions that enhance productivity and reduce operational downtime across East Africa and beyond.

  • Supply world-class products tailored to client needs
  • Ensure timely & efficient delivery, reducing downtime
  • Foster long-term partnerships with clients and global brands
  • Connect clients with state-of-the-art technologies

To be a global leader in providing advanced engineering solutions, industrial spare parts, and construction equipment, empowering businesses across industries with innovative and sustainable solutions.

  • Global leader in innovative engineering solutions
  • Sustainable supply chain for East Africa
  • Digital-first procurement platform
  • World-class after-sales support
