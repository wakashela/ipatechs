Genuine vs Counterfeit: Why It Matters

The cost of a counterfeit bearing isn't just the price on the invoice — it's the catastrophic failure six months later that destroys a gearbox worth USD 50,000 and halts production for three weeks. Tanzania's industrial sector has been flooded with counterfeit parts packaged in convincing reproductions of genuine-brand boxes. We've seen fake SKF bearings with substandard steel that spalls after weeks of service, and imitation Gates belts that stretch beyond tensioner limits within a single shift.

At IPATECHS, every part we supply is traceable to the manufacturer's production lot. We verify holographic seals, QR codes, and batch numbers before the component enters our inventory. Our quality assurance team inspects incoming shipments for packaging integrity, surface finish, dimensional accuracy, and material certification. When you buy spare parts East Africa through IPATECHS, you buy the peace of mind that only genuine OEM quality can deliver.
