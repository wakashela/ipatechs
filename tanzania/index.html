Nationwide Coverage Since 2019

IPATECHS Company Limited is a Tanzanian-owned industrial equipment supplier headquartered in Dar es Salaam, with logistics capabilities reaching every region of the United Republic of Tanzania. From the mining belts of Mwanza and Shinyanga to the agricultural heartlands of Morogoro and Iringa, from the tourist economy of Arusha and Kilimanjaro to the emerging industrial zones of Dodoma and Mbeya — we deliver industrial spare parts, engineering solutions, and construction equipment wherever our clients operate.

Since our founding in 2019, we have built a reputation as one of Tanzania's most reliable industrial spare parts suppliers. Our inventory covers bearings (SKF, FAG, Timken), electric motors (ABB, Siemens, Weg), pumps (Grundfos, Xylem, Flygt), hydraulic components (Parker, Gates), power transmission equipment, and mining machinery parts. We serve over 200 clients across the country, ranging from multinational mining operations to local manufacturing enterprises.

Supporting Tanzania's Industrial Growth

Tanzania is undergoing a significant industrial transformation. The government's Development Vision 2025 and the current Five-Year Development Plan prioritise infrastructure development, energy generation, and manufacturing capacity. As a local supplier with international reach, IPATECHS is positioned at the intersection of these priorities.

Our involvement in major national projects — including the Standard Gauge Railway (SGR), the Julius Nyerere Hydropower Project, port modernisation at Dar es Salaam, and the expansion of the natural gas infrastructure from Mtwara and Lindi — demonstrates our capability to deliver at scale. We provide PLC and SCADA automation systems, petroleum and gas equipment, and heavy construction machinery parts to contractors and government agencies executing these national priorities.
