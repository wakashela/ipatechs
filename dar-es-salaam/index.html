Dar es Salaam — Tanzania's Industrial Hub

Dar es Salaam is the commercial and industrial heart of Tanzania. Home to the country's largest port (TICTS), the majority of its manufacturing base, and the headquarters of most major corporations operating in East Africa, the city generates approximately 40% of Tanzania's GDP. IPATECHS is strategically located in Sinza, Makongo Road, placing us within 30 minutes of every major industrial zone in the city.

As Dar es Salaam's dedicated industrial spare parts supplier, we understand the urgency of downtime in a port city where every hour of stopped production costs money. Our same-day quote and delivery service means that procurement managers across DSM can have critical bearings, seals, hydraulic hoses, or electrical components delivered to their facility without waiting for international shipping. We stock over 50 product lines in our Sinza warehouse, with the most commonly requested items available for immediate collection or dispatch.

Industrial Zones We Serve in Dar es Salaam

Dar es Salaam's industry is concentrated in several distinct zones, each with specific equipment needs. Our location in Sinza enables rapid delivery to all of them:

  • Kurasini & Port Area — The TICTS container terminal, oil storage terminals, grain silos, and logistics warehouses. We supply port handling equipment parts, conveyor components, hydraulic systems for reach stackers and cranes, and petroleum & gas engineering equipment for the tank farms and pipeline operators along Mandela Road.
  • Nyerere Road Industrial Corridor — Running from the city centre towards the airport, this corridor hosts steel fabrication workshops, automotive service centres, packaging plants, plastics factories, and food processing facilities. Our mechanical engineering and electrical engineering products — motors, gearboxes, switchgear, VFDs — are in constant demand here.
  • Keko — A dense industrial area with a mix of light manufacturing, textile mills, printing presses, and metalworking shops. We supply bearings, belts, chains, sprockets, and power transmission components to the many small and medium enterprises operating in this zone.
  • Ubungo — Home to bus assembly plants, automotive parts distributors, and logistics companies serving the central corridor to Dodoma and beyond. Our automation equipment and construction machinery parts support the transport and infrastructure sectors concentrated here.
  • Mbezi & Mikocheni Industrial — Pharmaceutical manufacturing, food processing (beverages, dairy, edible oils), and consumer goods production. We supply stainless steel fittings, sanitary pumps, filtration systems, and corrosion-resistant materials for hygienic process environments.
  • Chang'ombe & Temeke — Cement bagging plants, quarry operations, and construction material suppliers. We provide crusher parts, screen media, conveyor belts, and heavy-duty bearings to keep aggregate and cement operations running.
