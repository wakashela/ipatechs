For power distribution, we supply transformers to IEC 60076 standards including distribution transformers (pole-mounted and ground-mounted, 11 kV/0.433 kV and 33 kV/0.433 kV), power transformers (up to 132 kV), and instrument transformers (current transformers CTs, voltage transformers VTs/Potential PTs). Switchgear, Protection & ATS

Safe, reliable power distribution depends on well-engineered switchgear and protection systems. IPATECHS supplies low-voltage and medium-voltage circuit breakers (air circuit breakers ACBs, moulded case circuit breakers MCCBs, vacuum circuit breakers VCBs) from Schneider Electric, Siemens, ABB, and Terasaki. Protective relays (overcurrent, earth fault, differential, distance) from Siemens Siprotec, ABB Relion, and Schneider Easergy families are available for new installations and retrofit of aging electromechanical protection. Surge arresters, instrument transformers, disconnect switches, and earthing systems complete the protection package.

For facilities that require uninterrupted power, we supply automatic transfer switches (ATS) from 63A to 3,200A in open-transition, closed-transition, and bypass-isolation configurations. These work in concert with our generator packages and our Electrical Engineering division to create complete standby power systems. We also supply synchronising panels and load-sharing controllers for multi-generator installations where N+1 redundancy is required at hospitals, data centres, and critical process plants.

UPS Systems, Load Banks & Fuel Systems

Uninterruptible power supplies (UPS) protect sensitive equipment from grid disturbances, and IPATECHS supplies online double-conversion UPS systems from 1 kVA to 800 kVA, with brands including APC by Schneider Electric, Eaton, and Huawei. These systems serve server rooms, hospital operating theatres, air traffic control equipment, and industrial PLC/DCS installations. We also supply replacement battery strings (VRLA and lithium) and maintenance bypass panels. Our load bank selection — resistive, reactive, and combined — enables generator commissioning and periodic testing at capacities from 50 kW to 3 MW, essential for ensuring standby power systems perform when called upon.

Fuel systems are the lifeblood of generator reliability. We supply bulk fuel storage tanks (double-wall, UL-listed), day tanks with integrated pump sets, fuel transfer pumps, fuel filtration and polishing systems, level sensors and gauging, and fuel management systems that track consumption and prevent theft. For clients with large generator fleets or remote installations, proper fuel system design significantly reduces maintenance costs and extends engine service life. This offering complements our Petroleum & Gas Engineering services, particularly for clients who require both fuel infrastructure and power generation in single-site projects.
